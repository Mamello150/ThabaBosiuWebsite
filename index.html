Welcome to the Thaba Bosiu Cultural Festival

Event Overview

Thaba Bosiu culture Festival is an annul celebration of Basotho Cultural and heritage. The festive takes place near the hestoric mountain of Thaba Bosiu where King Moshoeshoe 1 established the Basotho Nation.

Visitors experiance traditional music, dance, Basotho food, cultural fashion and storytelling. The festival attracts both local and international tourists.
